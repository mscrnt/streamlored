# Key Locations

## Raccoon City
Midwestern American city that was home to Umbrella Corporation's primary research facilities. Site of the T-Virus outbreak in September 1998. The city was destroyed by a nuclear missile on October 1, 1998 to contain the outbreak. Setting for RE2, RE3, and Outbreak games.

## Spencer Mansion
Located in the Arklay Mountains outside Raccoon City. Built by Ozwell E. Spencer, co-founder of Umbrella. Contains elaborate puzzles and secret laboratories. Site of the original game's events where S.T.A.R.S. investigated the "cannibal murders."

## Raccoon City Police Department (R.P.D.)
The police station built in a former art museum, hence its unusual architecture. Main setting of RE2. Chief Brian Irons was secretly on Umbrella's payroll. Features the iconic main hall with the goddess statue.

## The Village (RE8)
Remote Eastern European village controlled by Mother Miranda. Home to the Four Lords: Lady Dimitrescu, Donna Beneviento, Salvatore Moreau, and Karl Heisenberg. Located near Castle Dimitrescu and the Megamycete cavern.

## Baker Estate
Located in Dulvey, Louisiana. Home of the Baker family who were infected by Eveline's mold. Setting of RE7. Includes the main house, old house, testing area, and wrecked ship where Eveline originated.

## The Island (RE4)
Military island base where Saddler conducted Las Plagas research. Final area of RE4. Contains laboratories, military installations, and the dock where the final battle occurs.

## BSAA Headquarters
The Bioterrorism Security Assessment Alliance is an international anti-bioterrorism organization. Chris and Jill work for BSAA. Has branches worldwide to respond to bioterror incidents.
