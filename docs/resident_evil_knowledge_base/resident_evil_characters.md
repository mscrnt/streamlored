# Protagonists

## Chris Redfield
Former Air Force pilot and founding member of S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team. One of the series' primary protagonists appearing in RE1, RE5, RE6, RE7, and RE Village. Known for his strength, dedication to fighting bioterrorism, and his partnership with his sister Claire and fellow agent Jill Valentine. Later becomes a BSAA operative.

## Jill Valentine
S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team member and one of the original protagonists. Stars in RE1, RE3, and RE5. Expert in lockpicking and bomb disposal. She was infected with the T-Virus in RE3 but survived thanks to a vaccine. Was mind-controlled by Wesker in RE5.

## Leon S. Kennedy
Rookie police officer on his first day in Raccoon City during the outbreak (RE2). Becomes a U.S. government agent and stars in RE4 and RE6. Known for his one-liners and his mission to rescue the President's daughter Ashley Graham in RE4.

## Claire Redfield
Chris's younger sister and a skilled motorcyclist. Protagonist of RE2 and Code: Veronica. Works for TerraSave, a human rights organization. Known for her protective nature, especially toward children like Sherry Birkin.

## Ada Wong
Mysterious spy who works for various organizations. Recurring character starting in RE2. Has a complex relationship with Leon. Her true allegiances are always uncertain. Expert in infiltration and manipulation.

## Ethan Winters
Protagonist of RE7 and RE Village. Ordinary man searching for his wife Mia who was kidnapped by the Baker family. Later faces Mother Miranda to save his daughter Rose.

## Barry Burton
Barry Burton is a veteran member of the Raccoon City S.T.A.R.S. team, known for his expertise with firearms and his unwavering loyalty to his friends—especially Chris and Jill. A former SWAT officer with a strong moral compass, Barry is both physically imposing and emotionally grounded, often serving as the team’s dependable “big brother.” In the original _Resident Evil_, he’s manipulated by Albert Wesker through threats to his family, leading to moments of conflicted betrayal, but ultimately he redeems himself by aiding Jill and helping expose Umbrella’s schemes. Throughout the series, Barry is remembered for his reliability, dad-like charm, and unintentionally iconic one-liners (“You were almost a Jill sandwich!”), making him one of the franchise’s most beloved supporting characters. He is a playable main character in the non-canon Game Boy Color game Resident Evil: Gaiden, and the spin-off game Resident Evil: Revelations 2

# Antagonists
## Albert Wesker
Main antagonist of the series. Former S.T.A.R.S. captain who betrayed his team. Has superhuman abilities due to virus experimentation. Seeks global domination through viral evolution. Killed in RE5 in a volcano.
